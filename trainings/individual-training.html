Individual Training

About the training

Our 1-on-1 training begins with a evaluation session, which we use to create a personal development plan garnered towards the athlete’s needs.

This personal development plan will address the areas where the athlete can improve, while enhancing their strengths to develop an elite, well-rounded player. During and at the conclusion of the program, the athlete’s progress will be evaluated and the plan will be refined if necessary.

Training Features

Technical Training

Dribbling, passing, kicking, defending, 1st touch training.

Game Understanding

Tactical awareness, vision, movement off the ball.

Strength & Conditioning

Aerobic/anaerobic fitness, speed & agility, injury prevention, nutritional insight.
